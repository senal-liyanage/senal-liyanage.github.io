Research themes

Chemistry phenomena I like to make visible.

The technical work sits behind the scenes. The public portfolio focuses on motion, interfaces, organization, and molecular interactions that can be shown clearly.

Main visual themes

Phase behavior

Evaporation, diffusion, and molecular motion across liquid and vapor regions.

Soft-material interfaces

Polymer nanoparticles, hydration, solvent exposure, and interfacial organization.

Molecular recognition

Binding poses, contacts, docking snapshots, and interaction patterns.

Electronic structure

Spectra, molecular structure, and computational chemistry outputs turned into interpretable figures.

Portfolio style rule

Every project should answer one simple question: what is happening at the molecular scale, and how can we see it?

What I will handle here

Website structure, page layout, wording, visual framing, project organization, and public-facing presentation.

What belongs in each project

A clean animation or figure, a short chemistry story, a minimal method note, and a careful boundary around what the model can and cannot claim.
