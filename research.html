Research themes

Chemistry phenomena I like to make visible.

My work uses molecular simulation to study motion, interfaces, organization, and interactions. On this site, I keep the presentation visual and concise.

Themes I want to explore visually

Phase behavior

Evaporation, diffusion, and molecular motion across liquid and vapor regions.

Soft-material interfaces

Polymer nanoparticles, hydration, solvent exposure, and interfacial organization.

Molecular recognition

Binding poses, contacts, docking snapshots, and interaction patterns.

Electronic structure

Spectra, molecular structure, and computational chemistry outputs turned into interpretable figures.

How I want these pages to read

Each project should be easy to enter: one visual idea, one molecular explanation, and only the method details needed to understand what I am showing.
