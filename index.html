What I want this portfolio to show

Phenomena first

I want each project to start with a visual question: what is happening, and what does molecular motion look like?

Chemistry-aware visuals

The visuals should be simple, but not empty. I include enough chemistry to explain what is moving, changing, or interacting.

Readable methods

The method details are there for context, but they should support the visual story rather than turn the page into a report.
