# Project 001 captions

## Project title

When Hydrogen Bonds Let Go: Molecular Vaporization of Water

## Short caption

A TIP4P/2005 molecular-dynamics slab simulation visualized as a public-facing particle animation. Blue particles remain inside the original liquid band, while orange particles represent waters outside that band in the vapor/diffuse region.

## Website caption

At 300 K, the simulated water slab remains compact and liquid-like. As temperature increases, the interface broadens and more molecules populate the vapor region. The 600 K animation shows this qualitatively by tracking the growing population outside the original liquid-slab band.

## Reliability note

This is a qualitative molecular visualization from a finite NVT slab simulation. It is not a quantitative boiling-point, vapor-pressure, or surface-tension calculation.

## Alt text

Animated schematic of a vertical water slab simulation at 600 K. Blue points show water molecules inside the original liquid slab band, orange points show molecules outside the slab band, and dashed horizontal lines mark the original liquid boundaries.
